## Achievement system

Achievements are one-time goals that reward you with stardust bonuses when completed. The game checks your progress against all achievements during every run, and newly unlocked achievements trigger an in-game notification.

## All achievements

| Achievement | ID | Requirement | Stardust Reward |
|------------|-----|-------------|-----------------|
| First Flight | `first_flight` | Score 1 point | 10 |
| Getting Started | `getting_started` | Reach a score of 10 | 25 |
| Space Rookie | `space_rookie` | Reach a score of 25 | 50 |
| Asteroid Dodger | `asteroid_dodger` | Reach a score of 50 | 100 |
| Space Ace | `space_ace` | Reach a score of 75 | 150 |
| Half Minute Hero | `half_minute_hero` | Survive for 30 seconds | 30 |
| Close Call | `close_call` | Get 3 near-misses in one run | 40 |
| Rising Challenge | `rising_challenge` | Reach Hard difficulty | 50 |

### Total rewards

Completing all 8 achievements earns you **455 stardust** in total bonuses.

## Achievement categories

### Score achievements

These achievements track the highest score you reach in a single run. Score equals the number of obstacles passed.

| Achievement | Required Score | Reward |
|------------|---------------|--------|
| First Flight | 1 | 10 |
| Getting Started | 10 | 25 |
| Space Rookie | 25 | 50 |
| Asteroid Dodger | 50 | 100 |
| Space Ace | 75 | 150 |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Score achievements are cumulative in a single run. Reaching a score of 75 unlocks all five score achievements at once if none were previously completed.
</Callout>

### Survival achievement

| Achievement | Required Time | Reward |
|------------|--------------|--------|
| Half Minute Hero | 30 seconds | 30 |

### Near-miss achievement

| Achievement | Required Near-Misses | Reward |
|------------|---------------------|--------|
| Close Call | 3 in one run | 40 |

Near-misses are counted when you fly very close to an obstacle without colliding. You need 3 near-misses within a single run to unlock this achievement.

### Difficulty achievement

| Achievement | Required Tier | Effective Level | Reward |
|------------|--------------|-----------------|--------|
| Rising Challenge | Hard | 10+ | 50 |

The difficulty tier is determined by your effective difficulty level, which increases as you survive longer. Reaching an effective level of 10 or higher triggers Hard difficulty.

## How achievements are checked

The game evaluates all achievement conditions continuously during gameplay. When you meet a requirement:

1. The achievement is marked as completed
2. The stardust reward is added to your balance immediately
3. A notification callback fires for UI feedback
4. The achievement state is saved to persistent storage

<Callout kind="info">
  Achievements are checked against four parameters: current score, survival time, near-miss count in the current run, and current effective difficulty level.
</Callout>

## Viewing achievements

Open the achievements screen from the main menu to see all achievements with their completion status. The screen displays:

- A progress counter showing completed vs total (e.g., "3 / 8")
- Each achievement card with name, description, and stardust reward
- Completed achievements show a green checkmark and gold border
- Incomplete achievements show your current best progress toward the requirement
