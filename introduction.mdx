## Welcome to SpaceFlapper

SpaceFlapper is a space-themed arcade game where you pilot a jetpack-equipped astronaut through an ever-changing gauntlet of cosmic obstacles. Tap to flap, dodge hazards, collect stardust, and chase your high score across three distinct themed scenes.

Built with SpriteKit and SwiftUI for iOS, SpaceFlapper combines accessible tap-to-flap controls with layered mechanics that reward precision, streaks, and risk-taking.

## What makes SpaceFlapper unique

SpaceFlapper goes beyond the classic flappy formula with systems designed to keep every run feeling fresh:

- **Dynamic difficulty** that scales with your score and survival time
- **Near-miss rewards** that give bonus points for threading the needle, with escalating chain bonuses
- **Streak system** that tracks consecutive obstacle passes and triggers visual effects at higher levels
- **Six dynamic events** that transform gameplay mid-run, from meteor storms to gravity flips
- **Three power-ups** including shields, rocket boosts, and time warp
- **Stardust economy** for unlocking suits and tracking long-term progress
- **Ghost rival system** that races a replay of your previous best run

## Game at a glance

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Platform | iOS (SpriteKit + SwiftUI) |
| Controls | Tap anywhere to flap |
| Obstacle types | 12+ including static, moving, rotating, and gravity wells |
| Power-ups | Shield (15s), Rocket Boost (3s), Time Warp (4s) |
| Dynamic events | 6 types: Speed Surge, Warp Zone, Meteor Storm, Gravity Flip, Comet Ride, Stardust Fever |
| Scoring | 1 point per obstacle + near-miss bonuses + streak multipliers |
| Collectibles | Stardust (star bits) for the in-game shop |
| Progression | Achievements, suit unlocks, and persistent statistics |
