## Overview

SpaceFlapper awards points primarily for passing through obstacle gaps. Additional points come from near-miss bonuses, event survival rewards, and stardust collection. Multiple systems can multiply your score simultaneously.

## Base scoring

You earn **1 point** each time you successfully pass through an obstacle gap. A score zone is placed between each obstacle pair, and passing through it triggers the score increment.

## Score multipliers

Several mechanics can multiply the points you earn per obstacle pass.

| Source | Multiplier | Trigger condition |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Base scoring | 1x | Default |
| Gravity Flip | 2x | Active during inverted gravity |
| Stardust Fever | 3x | Triggered at streak level 4 (12 passes) |
| Speed Surge | Bonus point | Each pass during 2x speed |

<Callout kind="info">
  Multipliers from different events do not stack with each other. The highest active multiplier takes priority.
</Callout>

## Near-miss bonuses

Passing within **15-25 points** of an obstacle edge without colliding triggers a near-miss bonus. See the [near-miss detection page](/mechanics/near-miss) for details on detection zones and rewards.

## Event survival bonuses

Dynamic events award bonus points for survival or completion.

| Event | Bonus | Condition |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| Meteor Storm | +10 points | Survive without shield hit |
| Meteor Storm (shield used) | +5 points | Survive with shield absorbing a hit |
| Gravity Flip | +5 points | Survive the full 6-second flip |
| Warp Zone | +1 per 0.5s | Auto-score during 3-second warp |
| Comet Ride | +1 per shatter | Shattering obstacles while riding |

## Stardust collection

Stardust collectibles award a separate currency used for progression. Their value depends on tier:

| Tier | Stardust value | Spawn chance |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Gold | 3 | 10% |
| Silver | 2 | 30% |
| Bronze | 1 | 60% |

<Callout kind="tip">
  During Cosmic Magnet and Stardust Fever events, all collectibles spawn as gold tier, maximizing your stardust income.
</Callout>

## Difficulty and scoring

Score directly drives difficulty progression. Every **8 points**, the difficulty level increases by 1. This means higher scores bring faster obstacle speeds, smaller gaps, and more frequent moving obstacles. See the [difficulty scaling page](/mechanics/difficulty) for the full curve.

## High score tracking

The game persists your all-time high score locally. Your current score is displayed during gameplay and compared against your record at game over.
